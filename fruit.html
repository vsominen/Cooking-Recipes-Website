Sweet Lime Summer Fruit Salad

INGREDIENTS: 

INGREDIENTS: Fruit Salad

1 mango, peeled and cubed

1 cup (150g) fresh blueberries 

1 cup (150 g) purple grapes 

1/2 cup (75 g) fresh strawberries, quartered 

2 kiwis, sliced or cut into cubes 

 

INGREDIENTS: Sweet Lime Dressing

1/2 lime, juice and zest (~1 Tbsp or 15 mL)

2 tsp (5 mL) agave syrup (optional)

 

Directions

Add all fruit to a bowl. 

In a small bowl, whisk together the agave syrup and lime juice, then add to the fruit and toss to combine. Serve immediately.
 Enjoy!

Notes

Variations: add any fruit of your choosing, such as raspberries, oranges, apples or pears.

Storage: store in an air-tight container in the fridge for up to one day; if possible, 
it’s best to store the dressing separately and mix with fruit when ready to serve to keep the fruit fresh/crisp.
